Scrambled Eggs

Learn how to make scrambled eggs! With this 5-ingredient recipe, they come out soft, creamy, and flavorful every time. An easy, delicious breakfast.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Milk, plant milk or water
  • Extra virgin olive oil, or butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Instructions

  1. 1. Beat the eggs
  2. 2. Place them in a medium bowl
  3. 3. Whisk until the yolk and whites are thoroughly combined
  4. 4. Add the milk or water, and whisk again!
  5. 5. Gently, preheat the pan
  6. 6. Brush a small nonstick skillet with olive oil, or melt a little butter inside it.
  7. 7. Warm the skillet over medium heat.
  8. 8. Finally, cook. Pour in the egg mixture, and let it cook for a few seconds, undisturbed.
